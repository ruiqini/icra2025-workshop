Physics-informed Robot Learning for Motion Planning and Control

ICRA 2025 Workshop

May 27, 2025

Overview

This workshop will explore the integration of Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) with robotics, focusing on how PDE-driven principles can enhance robot learning algorithms. It aims to bring together experts from robot learning, planning, and computational mathematics to develop efficient methods for motion planning and control in complex environments.
